Eatly

AI-Powered Smart Diet Planner

❓ The Problem: Fragmented Dieting

Most health enthusiasts face three main barriers when trying to maintain a healthy lifestyle:

  • Generic Advice: Standard calorie targets that don't account for individual body composition or activity levels.
  • Manual Tracking: The tedious task of logging every ingredient and calculating macros manually.
  • Food Waste: Difficulty in finding recipes that use the ingredients already in the fridge.

🛠️ The Build: Intelligent Nutrition

AI Vision

Gemini Pro Vision for instant ingredient recognition from photos.

Secure Auth

Supabase for authentication and persistent data sync.

Smart Planning

Gemini Pro for personalized meal plans.

Dynamic UI

Vite + React for a lightning-fast experience.

Eatly is a full-stack health platform that leverages Google's Gemini AI to bridge the gap between "having ingredients" and "having a plan." By combining Supabase's real-time capabilities with advanced LLMs, it automates the most painful parts of dieting.

🧠 Technical Deep Dive: Vision to Meal-Plan

The core of Eatly is its dual AI integration. First, a **Vision Pipeline** identifies food items from user images with 90%+ accuracy. Second, a **Personalized Recommendation Engine** maps these ingredients against user biometrics.

The system calculates precise daily targets (Calories, Protein, Carbs, Fats) based on weight, height, and goal, then prompts Gemini to generate a complete 3-day meal plan and a consolidated grocery list, formatted as structured JSON for immediate UI rendering.

✨ The Results

  • Zero-Effort Planning: AI-generated meal plans tailored to individual health goals.
  • Smart Groceries: Automated, consolidated shopping lists that minimize waste and cost.
  • Engagement: Built-in streak tracking to keep users motivated on their fitness journey.
